Effective EAL teaching strategies can significantly enhance the learning experience for students. Some key strategies include:

Visual Aids: Use images, charts, and videos to support understanding.

Scaffolding: Break down tasks into smaller, manageable steps to build confidence and skills.

Interactive Activities: Engage students with group work, discussions, and hands-on activities to promote language use.

About William Tame

I specialize in guiding students through the academic and social aspects of learning English as an additional language (EAL).

My rich teaching experience spanning over 15 years within international Early Years and Primary School settings has equipped me with a deep understanding of diverse learning needs.

As an accomplished collaboration leader, I'm experienced at coordinating language assessment, implementing effective teaching strategies, and facilitating professional learning opportunities.

"Once upon a time..."

Through the magic of words and storytelling, I create vibrant and captivating narratives that not only entertain but also inspire young minds.

I enhance storytelling by providing a multi-sensory experience for my audience. Whether it's weaving tales of adventure, instilling valuable life lessons, or sparking the imagination with fantastical worlds, I draw quick illustrations, or use props and puppets to help visualize the story.

By incorporating interactive elements and encouraging creativity, I strive to make storytelling an enriching and memorable experience for young learners, fostering a love for literature and the joy of exploration within their hearts."
