About William Tame

I specialize in guiding students through the academic and social aspects of learning English as an additional language (EAL).

As a Global EAL Collaboration Leader, I'm experienced at coordinating language assessment, implementing effective teaching strategies, and facilitating professional learning opportunities.

My rich teaching experience spanning over 14 years in teaching EAL within international school settings has equipped me with a deep understanding of diverse learning needs.
